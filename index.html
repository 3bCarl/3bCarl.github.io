How Cyber Secure Is Your Business?

Many organisations believe they're fully protected. However, hidden weaknesses, both big and small, often exist beneath the surface. Unseen vulnerabilities can be a goldmine for cyber criminals. It’s crucial that these are identified and remediated before they are exploited.


Open-Source Threat Assessment: What’s Included?

Website Security Review - We conduct a high-level examination of your website's security posture to identify any glaring risks or areas for improvement.

Leaked Credentials Scan - We search the surface and dark web to find out if your credentials have been compromised.

Vulnerability Analysis - We identify potential website vulnerabilities that hackers could exploit.

Subdomain & Portal Discovery - Hidden doesn't mean safe. We unearth exposed subdomains and portals that could be gateways for attackers.

Public Data Assessment - Publicly accessible data, if not managed correctly, can be a treasure trove for cyber criminals. We assess this data to highlight potential vulnerabilities.


Publicly Available Data – And Nothing More

We strictly analyse information that is already out in the open. This means we don’t dig into private records, hidden databases, or any unauthorised spaces.
Our goal is to identify vulnerabilities from data that is publicly accessible, showing you potential risks from what's already visible to anyone online.
We guarantee that at no point do we attempt to gain unauthorised access to any of your websites, systems, or accounts.
Our methods are completely above board, aligning with best practices in cyber security and ethical guidelines.
